3.5.2. Biological Opinion RPAs

The standards listed here are from the Fish and Wildlife Service (FWS) and National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) Biological Opinions on operations of the CVP and SWP.  The specific standards here are Reasonable and Prudent Alternatives (RPAs) described in those Biological Opinions.  See Appendix B in the User's Reference Guide for more description of these RPAs.  As with D-1641 regulations, activating the appropriate checkbox will turn on the standard.  There are no user-defined options for these RPAs.

The RPAs on this tab are:

-Old and Middle River (FWS RPA Actions 1-3). This limits CVP and SWP exports so that flow in the Old and Middle River is no more negative than a specified standard during Dec-June.  Depending on the time of year and hydrological and biological conditions this standard can vary from -1,250 to -5,000 cfs.

-Fall X2 (FWS RPA Action 4).  This sets X2 standards for Sept-Nov, which are applied in Wet and Above Normal water years.

-Clear Creek (NMFS RPA Action 1.1.1).  This sets minimum flow standards in Clear Creek.

-Delta Cross Channel (NMFS RPA Action 4.1.2). This defines the number of days that the DCC gates are open in each month.  When both this and the D-1641 DCC standard are active, the RPA gate operations are layered on top of the D-1641 gate operations.

-San Joaquin River Inflow to Export Ratio (NMFS RPA Action 4.2.1). This limits the combined exports of CVP and SWP to a fraction of San Joaquin River at Vernalis, which varies by water year type.
