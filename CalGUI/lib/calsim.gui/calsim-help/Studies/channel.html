Channel Arc Table

Channel capacities and minimum flows are specified in this table. For most channel arcs the minimum flow will be zero. A negative value implies that the flow may occur in two directions. A value in the minimum flow data field will create two split arc components (C#_MIF and C#_EXC), and apply an upper bound of the minimum flow value on the MIF arc. A "NONE" in the minimum flow data field will prevent the arc from being split.
