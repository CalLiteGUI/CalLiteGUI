Math Functions

The Math functions are available from the Tools menu or  Math Functions Toolbar button and are organized into six categories: Arithmetic, General, Time Functions, Hydrologic, Smoothing, and Statistics.  Each category is a tab on the Math Functions screen (see below).  This chapter describes math functions according to these categories.
