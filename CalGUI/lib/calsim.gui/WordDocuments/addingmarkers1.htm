Adding Markers

To add a marker:

 

  1. Right-click on the location in the plot where you want the marker to appear.
  2. From the Viewport shortcut menu (see below), point to Add Marker, and then click either On X-Axis or On Y-Axis.

 

The marker will now appear in the plot.
