Customizing the Display of Tabular Data

From the table window’s View menu, you can choose to display commas in numbers by selecting the Commas command.  You can revere the order of the table by selecting the View → Reverse Order command.  For time series data, this option will show the latest values first.  The date and time of the data can be tabulated in separate columns by selecting Date and Time Separately.  To have the dates display the years with four digits, select Date With 4 Digit Years.  You can set the precision of decimal places for your data by selecting Show Decimal Places and selecting the number of decimal places you wish to display, see figure below.  You can also change how missing values are displayed by selecting Show Missing As and then by choosing blanks (the default), “-901.0”, “M”, or “-M-”.
