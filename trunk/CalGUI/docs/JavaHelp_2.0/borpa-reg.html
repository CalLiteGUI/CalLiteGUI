3.5.2. Biological Opinion RPAs Tab

The standards listed on this tab are from the Fish and Wildlife Service (FWS) and National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) Biological Opinions on operations of the CVP and SWP, released in Dec. 2008 and June 2009 respectively.  The specific standards here are Reasonable and Prudent Alternatives (RPAs) described in those Biological Opinions.  See Appendix B in the CalLite Reference Manual for more description of these RPAs.  As with D-1641 regulations, activating the appropriate checkbox will turn on the standard.  

While there are no user-defined options for these RPAs, for three of them there is a user-defined standard on another tab that could conflict with the RPA standard.  This occurs for the Old and Middle River RPA (see the Other Regulation tab) and the Fall X2 and Delta Cross Channel RPAs (see the D-1641 tab).  To reduce confusion about which regulatory standard to apply, these three RPA standards are always automatically deactivated if the corresponding standard is user-defined, even if the RPA checkbox is checked, and in those cases only the user-defined standards apply.   

The RPAs on this tab are:

-Old and Middle River (FWS RPA Actions 1-3). This limits CVP and SWP exports so that flow in the Old and Middle River is no more negative than a specified standard during Dec-June.  Depending on the time of year and hydrological and biological conditions this standard can vary from -1,250 to -5,000 cfs.  

-Fall X2 (FWS RPA Action 4).  This sets X2 standards for Sept-Nov, which are applied in Wet and Above Normal water years.  Since this RPA applies in different months than the D-1641 X2 standard, both can be applied in the same scenario.

-Clear Creek (NMFS RPA Action 1.1.1).  This sets minimum flow standards in Clear Creek.

-Delta Cross Channel (NMFS RPA Action 4.1.2). This defines the number of days that the DCC gates are open in each month.  DCC operations under this RPA will always have no more days open than the D-1641 DCC standard, and in some months may have even fewer days open.  Since this RPA was crafted as extra protection above and beyond the D-1641 standard, it will operate identically whether or not the D-1641 DCC standard is explicitly activated in the GUI.   

-San Joaquin River Inflow to Export Ratio (NMFS RPA Action 4.2.1). This limits the combined exports of CVP and SWP to a fraction of San Joaquin River at Vernalis, which varies by water year type.

WARNING: Any user defined values will be used in place of the Biological Opinion regulations. This applies to X2, Old Middle River, and San Joaquin River Inflow/Export Index user defined options.
