3.5.1. D-1641 Regulations

The regulations that are selectable on this tab are all from the State Water Resources Control Board Decision 1641 (D-1641).  These regulations are summarized below, but for a more detail on the D-1641 standards, see Appendices B and C of the User's Reference Manual. Clicking the checkbox next to a regulation will activate it for the current CalLite scenario.  For all of these regulations except the salinity standards and the pulse period export cap based on Vernalis flow, D-1641 standards can be replaced with user-defined standards.  If this is desired, after turning on the regulation, click on the radio button for user defined and enter desired values in the editable table (on the right half of the screen).  The month numbers in the user-defined tables refer to water year months, so Oct = 1, Nov =2 etc.     

The regulations on this tab are as follows:

-Delta Cross Channel (DCC).  This defines the numbers of days that the DCC gates are open in each month.  Operation of the DCC assists in transferring fresh water from the Sacramento River across the Delta.  Operations depend on water quality, flood protection, and fish and wildlife requirements.

-Sacramento River at Rio Vista Minimum Flow.  This defines minimum flows at Rio Vista, which vary by month and water year type.

-Minimum Net Delta Outflow.  This defines minimum outflows through the Delta.  User-defined values can be set by month and water year type.

-X2 Requirements.  This defines the distance (in km) from the Golden Gate Bridge of the X2 position (salinity of 2 parts per thousand) in the Delta.  The checkbox for Roe Trigger can be used to turn on or off the D-1641 standard for days that X2 is west of Roe Island, which is enforced when the previous months X2 position was west of Roe Island.  When user-defined the regulation can be turned on or off and values can be set by month and water year type, and there is no Roe Trigger.

-Export-Inflow Ratio.  This limits the combined exports of CVP and SWP to a specific proportion of total Delta outflow.  When user-defined this proportion (ratio) can be adjusted by month.

-Vernalis-Based Export Restriction.  This limits the combined exports of CVP and SWP to the maximum of 1500 cfs or San Joaquin River flow at Vernalis.  This restriction only applies during the Apr 15-May 15 pulse period. 

-Salinity Standards at Emmaton and Jersey Point (for agriculture), Rock Slough (for M&I), and Collinsville (for fish and wildlife).
