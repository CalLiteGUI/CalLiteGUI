Multiple time-series (MTS)

Multi Time-Series (MTS) is a special derived series, it consists of a single time stamp and multiple values belonging to different time series but with the same time stamp.
    The representation of such a MTS would be a JTable with no operators at all in the first column. The  displays associated with such an MTS would be multi-data table and graph.

MTS can be manipulated in a similar way of manipulating DTS.
